Paneer Sabji Recepie

Description

TPaneer Sabji is a delicious and versatile Indian dish made with soft paneer cubes cooked in a richly spiced gravy. It's a popular vegetarian option, perfect for serving with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • Paneer Cubes
  • Oil
  • Curd
  • Ginger
  • Green Chili
  • Masala

Steps

  1. Get Paneer Cubes.
  2. Heat Ghee and roast the masalas.
  3. Put the paneer cubes in the roasted masalas.
  4. Server after 5 minutes.
